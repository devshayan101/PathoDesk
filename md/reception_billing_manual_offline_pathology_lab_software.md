# RECEPTION & BILLING MANUAL
## Offline Pathology Lab Software

**Audience:** Receptionists, Front-Office Staff  
**Purpose:** To ensure fast, accurate patient registration, billing, and sample handover without clinical risk.

---

## 1. Introduction

Reception staff are the **first point of contact** for patients. Your accuracy affects:
- Patient identity
- Billing correctness
- Sample traceability

This system is designed to be **simple and safe** if used correctly.

---

## 2. Reception Role Boundaries

### What Reception CAN Do
- Register patients
- Create test orders
- Generate bills and receipts
- Print sample labels

### What Reception CANNOT Do
- Enter or edit lab results
- Edit reference ranges
- Finalize reports

---

## 3. Patient Registration

### Steps
1. Go to **Patients → New Patient**
2. Enter:
   - Full name (spelling matters)
   - Date of birth / age
   - Gender
   - Phone number
3. Save patient

**Always confirm name spelling verbally.**

---

## 4. Order Creation

1. Open patient record
2. Click **Create Order**
3. Select tests/packages
4. Confirm sample types

System auto-generates:
- Order ID
- Sample IDs

---

## 5. Billing & Payment

### Invoice Generation
- Prices are auto-filled
- Discounts require approval (if enabled)

### Payment Modes
- Cash
- Card (record only)
- Credit (if allowed)

Always issue a receipt.

---

## 6. Printing Sample Labels

- Print barcode labels immediately after billing
- Attach labels before sample collection
- Never handwrite sample IDs

---

## 7. Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Creating duplicate patients
- Wrong gender or DOB
- Giving wrong labels
- Skipping receipt printing

All actions are audit-logged.

---

## 8. End-of-Day Checklist

- All bills printed
- No pending orders
- Cash matched with system

---
