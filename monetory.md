Here are the best ways you can benefit monetarily from this application:

1. Subscription-Based Licensing (SaaS Model)
Instead of selling the software for a one-time fee, you can charge labs a recurring fee. Since you have a license generation script, you can easily issue licenses that expire after a certain period (e.g., exactly 1 year from the issue date).

Monthly Subscription: e.g., $30 – $50 / month per lab.
Annual Subscription: e.g., $300 – $500 / year per lab (often paid upfront with a discount).
Benefits: This creates a steady, recurring stream of passive income. When their license expires, the app can prompt them to renew to continue using it.
2. Tiered Pricing (Freemium or Basic/Pro/Enterprise)
You can lock certain advanced features behind different license tiers:

Basic Tier ($): Patient management, basic test entries, simple printing.
Pro Tier ($$): Adds Doctor Commission tracking, advanced dashboard calculations (revenue vs. pending amounts), and discount logic functionality.
Enterprise Tier ($$$): Multi-computer networking (if supported), priority technical support, and automated data backups.
3. One-Time Perpetual License + Maintenance Fee
If labs in your local market are hesitant to pay subscriptions, you can sell a lifetime license for a much larger upfront cost (e.g., $800 - $1,500). To ensure continuous revenue, you can include 1 year of free updates/support and then charge an Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) of $100 - $200/year for continued tech support and feature updates.

4. Setup, Training, and Data Entry Fees
Software is only as good as the data inside it. Many doctors or lab technicians do not have the time to type out hundreds of tests and prices into the "Test Master".

You can charge an onboarding fee (e.g., $100 - $300) to physically go to their lab, install the software, set up their letterheads/logos, and import their custom Price List into the software for them.
5. Customization and White-Labeling
Every lab has specific workflows. Once a lab starts using your software, they will likely ask for custom features (like a specific new PDF format, SMS integration for patients, or WhatsApp results delivery).

Custom Features: You can charge by the hour or a flat rate to build these custom features.
White-labeling: You can charge a premium (e.g., $2,000+) to compile a customized version of the app where the app icon, title, and all branding are replaced with the specific Lab's branding instead of "PathoDesk".
